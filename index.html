The remarkable growth of Nigeria's tech ecosystem, renowned for its unmatched potential and vibrant creativity, has inspired GITEX, independently rated as the Best Tech Event in the World, to launch its legacy in this dynamic nation.

GITEX NIGERIA is your gateway to the nation’s dynamic digital transformation, offering unparalleled opportunities to engage with Nigeria’s booming tech landscape.

This landmark event redefines the standard by spanning two influential cities — Abuja, the seat of power, and Lagos, the pulse of West Africa’s economy. Join us at Transcorp Hilton, Abuja from 1-2 September and Eko Hotel Convention Centre, Lagos from 2-4 September for an unforgettable experience.
